🔹 ABOUT WHO YEMEN

The World Health Organization in Yemen coordinates health responses for millions in conflict-affected regions. We are committed to saving lives, preventing disease outbreaks, and strengthening the health system even in the most challenging situations.

Mission: To deliver lifesaving healthcare services to all vulnerable populations in Yemen through coordination, accountability, and rapid response.

  • Epidemic surveillance
  • Nutrition and maternal-child health
  • Emergency preparedness
  • Mental health and trauma recovery

🔹 HEALTH PROGRAMS & OPERATIONS

📌 Emergency Medical Services

Deploying mobile clinics and field teams to underserved regions.

📌 Vaccination Campaigns

Nationwide polio and measles immunizations reaching children under 5.

📌 Nutrition & Child Health

Screening malnourished children, delivering therapeutic feeding support.

📌 Disease Outbreak Response

Rapid-response teams containing cholera, dengue, and malaria outbreaks.
